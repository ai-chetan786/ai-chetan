About AI Chetan

India's first all-in-one AI assistant — built to help every Indian with legal, medical, tech, and daily life problems in their own language.

Our Story

AI Chetan was born from a simple idea — every Indian deserves access to smart, affordable help. Whether it's understanding a legal notice, knowing which medicine to take, fixing a phone problem, or just needing someone to talk to at midnight — AI Chetan is here.

We saw that most AI tools are made for the West, in English only, and are too expensive for the average Indian. So we built something different — something for Bharat. Supports Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, Bengali and more!
